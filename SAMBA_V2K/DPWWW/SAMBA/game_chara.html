Character introduction
Introducing some fun samba friends!

[Amigo]

A monkey who likes maracas. The main character of this game. Always in a good mood!
[Amiga]

Speaking of new characters, here's Amigo's younger sister, Amiga! Isn't she cute?
[Linda]

A dancer and a real idol in town. Amigo also seems to have fallen in love with her.
[Bingo & Bongo]

They think that no one knows the true identities of the people inside the disguises, but everyone knows.
[Rio]

Despite his effeminate appearance, Rio is actually a man... Did you notice it?
[Chumba & Wamba]

Former gang leader and bartender. They play mandolin and conga.
[Pose]

Who is this shadowy mastermind shrouded in a veil of mystery? He always has an authoritative tone, saying things like "Dance!" or "Strike a pose!". Plus, he seems to be pretty good at volleyball.
