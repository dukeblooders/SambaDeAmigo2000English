Precautions when using BBS

      		
     

     
 
      
     

     
 
      BBS is a public bulletin board system created for the purpose of exchanging information. Many people will use this service, so please pay attention to the following points to not disturb others. 
        
       
 
       
    
        
  1. Please refrain from making vulgar or defamatory comments likely to offend users.  
         
    
        
  2. Please refrain from posting personal topics that do not fit the purpose of each BBS or meaningless posts.  
         
    
        
  3. Make sure to write a title in a easy to understand way./FONT>  
         
    
        
  4. Avoid making new comments that are just greetings.  
         
    
        
  5. Check if the same question you are about to write has already been posted.  
         
    
        
  6. BBS is used by many beginners and young people on the Internet. Please respond kindly to inappropriate comments.
    7. 
       

       
If the above precautions are not followed, posts may be deleted or comments banned. Please follow precautions and make BBS pleasant to everyone.
