Lesson 7
Mini-game strategy

1. Master your receiving skill!
Do you know how to receive? You'll get the ball if you wait, but if you shake your maracas at the right time, the ball will go higher, making it easier to follow up with your next attack. Additionally, if you master receiving, you will be able to counter your opponent's power attacks!
2. Use power attacks!
The trick to a power attack is to decide which hand will receive the ball and which one will smash, then hit the ball repeatedly! Once you get used to it, you should be able to smash with all your might!
3. A foolproof block!
When your opponent attacks, you can block the shot by waiting in the air just above the net and shaking your maracas at the right moment!
4. Feint!
If the opponent is preparing to block, try a feint by lobbing rather than shooting. It's especially annoying in a match against another player!
5. More advanced techniques
There are still many techniques for this game, especially when playing against a friend in VS mode. Try to develop your own original techniques, such as a sudden double attack, feint a shot, etc.
